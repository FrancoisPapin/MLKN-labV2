## MLKN.lab: Standards Alignment

This project's structure is aligned with the following international classification systems and scientific literature standards:

## Core Layers

| Layer | Name | Alignment | Purpose |
|-------|------|-----------|---------|
| 1 | Core Discipline Domains | OECD Frascati (1.0–6.0), UNESCO (11xx–6.0) | High-level scientific domains |
| 2 | Academic Disciplines | OECD Frascati (1.1–6.4), UNESCO (4-digit), Scopus Subject Areas | Established academic fields |
| 3 | Academic Subdisciplines | OECD Frascati Subcategories, UNESCO (6-digit), Scopus Sub-Areas | Specialized subfields |
| 4 | Core Thematic Domains | Research Topics (Scopus, Web of Science), Cognitive Science Frameworks | Thematic clusters |
| 5 | Main Thematics | Scopus Keywords, MeSH Terms, IEEE Thesaurus | Fundamental concepts |

## Meta-Layers

| Meta-Layer | Categories | Alignment | Purpose |
|------------|------------|-----------|---------|
| Epistemic Layers | Basic Research, Applied Research, Experimental Development, Normative Research, Meta-Research | NSF, OECD | Knowledge production methods |
| Levels of Analysis | Molecular–Neural, Individual, Population, Social–Cultural, System–Global | WHO, CDC, Systems Theory | Scales of inquiry |
| Paradigms | Reductionist, Holistic, Critical | Kuhn, Lakatos | Underlying frameworks |
| Standards Alignment | OECD Frascati, UNESCO, Scopus, MeSH, IEEE, Web of Science | International standards | Classification alignment |
