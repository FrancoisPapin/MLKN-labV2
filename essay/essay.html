Essays Overview

Theoretical and Applied Explorations

Our essays explore the applications and theoretical implications of MLKN-lab. They are divided into two categories:

⬢

Meta-Essays

These meta-essays provide the philosophical and methodological foundation of the platform. They explain why MLKN-lab exists, how knowledge should be represented, and what kind of scientific reading the system makes possible.

⬢

Applied Essays

These applied essays represent the operational essays, tied directly to your data, tools, and visualizations. They read like scientific case studies or platform papers, not just conceptual reflections.
